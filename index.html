G Madegowda Institute of Technology

About Us

The Bharathi Education Society which subsequently became the Bharathi Education Trust has its headquarters at BET campus, Bharathinagara, MaddurTaluk, Karnataka. It was founded in 1962 by Sri G. Madegowda, the honourable ex-member of parliament and former minister, Govt. of Karnataka.

Hostels

To meet the needs of the student’s two separate well equipped hostels for men and women are maintained by the Trust. The hostels have separate facilities for North-Indian and South-Indian students.

Play Ground

The campus at Bharathinagara also has a big playfield and an open-air theatre where students get ample opportunities to hone their skills.

Gandhi Bhavan

A unique feature of the campus is the “Gandhi Bhavana” which has a collection of rare photographs, depicting the life of the Mahathma. The building houses a library and a conference hall. It is in keeping with the pronounced goal of B.E.T. to provide value based education at an affordable cost and inculcating among students Gandhian values.

G Madegowda Institute of Technology

G Madegowda Institute of Technology (GMIT) was established in the year 2011 by Bharathi Education Trust (BET) to fulfil the growing needs of the industry and aspirations of young students of Mandya District and other parts of the country, to cater this it requires outstanding professionals who can be an asset to any organization. The campus spreads over an area of 27 acres and located amidst lush greenery and a serene ambience in Bharathinagara. Institute has experienced and dedicated faculty, Good Infrastructure, Modern Equipment, state-of-the-art laboratories, Digital library, Playground, Swimming Pool, Transportation facilities provides the right place to the students for effective learning. GMIT is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi and approved by AICTE, New Delhi. G Madegowda Institute of Technology (GMIT) one of the premier institution started in the year 2011 with CSE, EEE, ECE, Civil and Mechanical branches by Bharathi Education Trust (BET), Bharathinagar. Since then, it has grown with time. At present, the institution runs 5 under graduate courses in Bachelor of Engineering (BE) 2 Post graduation courses in master of technology ( M.Tech) with an annual intake of about 415 students. The institution began with the mission of preparing the young generation for “Integration & Prosperity”. Today more than thousand students are studying in this institute & they are guided by more than 60 well qualified & experienced faculty members who work to integrate the mission of the college by their teaching training and research methods.

The college has adopted"Knowledge is Empowering"its theme & all the programs are focused on this theme. G Madegowda Institute of Technology (GMIT) has taken a leading role to uphold the entrepreneurial culture in Mandya District to motivate & guide young entrepreneurs, by giving consultancy & supporting services. GMIT is located in Bharathinagar, Mandya, Karnataka which is close to district Head Quarters, is at a distance of 90 km from Bangalore on SH-17 and is well connected by public transport and college buses. It has salubrious climate similar to that of Bangalore. The district is famous for many historical and tourist places.

What else?

Our VISION is to provide value based higher education to rural students at a very affordable cost and to make them responsive to the needs of the society and to make them useful citizens by inculcating in them Gandhian principles.

Transport

A full-fledged Transport department functions in the college with buses to provide transport facility to students and staff from various places. This service is offered ensuring a hassle-free and safe transportation.

Placement & Training

The Placement department is an integral part of GMIT connecting students of the various departments. The department has the best infrastructure for the smooth conduction of training and placement activities.

Sports & Co-Curricular

Students are trained under the guidance of qualified Physical Education Trainer & have achieved excellence in District level in sports activities. Industrial visits, Skills development programmes, National conference “G’Tech” are conducted Every year
